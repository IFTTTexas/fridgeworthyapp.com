Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026 · Applies to the Fridgeworthy iOS app

The short version: Fridgeworthy collects no data. None. Everything the app does happens on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Fridgeworthy has no accounts, no sign-in, no server, and no analytics. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information — yours or your kids'.

Calendar access

Fridgeworthy asks for access to your device calendars so it can build each day sheet. Calendar data is read on your device, used on your device, and never leaves your device. We cannot see it.

Calendar links you add

If you add a calendar link (for example a school portal calendar or a sports team feed), your device fetches that link directly from its source, the same way a calendar app would. That request goes from your phone to your school or team's server — never through us, because there is no "us" in the middle.

Purchases

The Fridgeworthy Family upgrade and tips are one-time purchases handled entirely by Apple's App Store. Apple processes the payment and manages the receipt under Apple's privacy policy. We never see your payment details.

Kids

Fridgeworthy is made for families. Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If a future feature ever requires anything beyond "no data collected," this policy will be updated first and the change will be called out plainly — not buried.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@fridgeworthyapp.com. I'm a dad who built this for my own fridge. I read everything.
